Methodology: SPIDER Framework​

graph LR
    S[Scope] --> P[Probe]

SPIDER - Systematic Problem Investigation and Design Enhancement Routine

  • Scope: Define boundaries and objectives
  • Probe: Deep dive investigation and analysis
  • Investigate: Root cause analysis and research
  • Design: Solution architecture and planning
  • Execute: Implementation and delivery
  • Review: Validation and continuous improvement
